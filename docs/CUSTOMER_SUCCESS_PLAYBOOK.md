# Customer Success Playbook

**Onboarding, retention, and expansion strategy for 7 revenue streams**

---

## Executive Summary

**Why CS Matters**: 50% of revenue comes from retention, not acquisition.

- **Churn Cost**: Lose 1 customer = lose 12 months of revenue
- **Expansion Opportunity**: Existing customer 5x easier to upsell than new customer
- **Retention Rule**: Every 5% reduction in churn = 25-40% increase in LTV

**This Playbook Covers**:
1. Day 1-7 onboarding for each stream
2. 30/60/90-day check-ins
3. Expansion triggers (upsell opportunities)
4. Churn prevention playbook
5. Customer success metrics dashboard

### Churn Recovery Script (If Customer Says They Want to Leave)

```
WHEN YOU HEAR "We want to cancel":

STEP 1: DON'T PANIC - Thank them
"Thank you for letting me know. I want to understand what happened."

STEP 2: ASK - Uncover root cause
"Help me understand - what's driving this decision?"
Listen. Don't interrupt. Ask follow-up questions.

STEP 3: VALIDATE - Show you understand
"I hear you - [rephrasing their concern]. That makes sense."

STEP 4: EXPLORE - Are there alternatives?
"Before we cancel, can I ask - would [Alternative] work?
   - Lower tier price?
   - Different product fit?
   - Extended trial of [Feature]?
   - Different contract term?

STEP 5: FINAL OFFER - Show you value them
"I really think we can solve this. Here's what I can offer:
   - [Discount to lower tier]
   - [Additional feature free for 3 months]
   - [Extended support / extra help]"

STEP 6: DECISION - Respect their choice
If they still want to leave:
"I understand. I'll process the cancellation.
 Two asks:
 1. Can I ask what you'll use instead? (Learning)
 2. Would you be open to reconnecting in 6 months? (Future)

 Thank you for trying us. We hope to work together again."

STEP 7: FOLLOW-UP - Exit interview
After cancellation, send email:
"We miss you! To help others avoid similar issues, would you
spend 5 min on this quick survey?"
â†’ Survey about what didn't work
â†’ What would bring you back?
â†’ Any feedback on our support?

STEP 8: TRACK - Learn from it
Log in churn database:
- Customer: [Name]
- Reason: [Primary reason for churn]

- Reactivation: [Could we win them back?]
- Learning: [What to do differently next time]
```
