## üí° Key Insights

### 1. Revenue Diversification Reduces Risk
Instead of depending on one stream, build 7 complementary revenue sources:
- Support + Training = $162K (foundation)
- ggen SaaS = $136K (platform revenue)
- Consulting = $50K (services revenue)
- Others = $80K (emerging streams)

### 2. Launch Order Matters
Start with low-effort streams (support, training) to generate cash, then use that cash to fund complex streams (ggen, enterprise).

## ‚ùì FAQ

**Q: Do I need to build all 7 streams?**
A: No. Start with 3 (support, training, consulting). Add others as you get traction.

**Q: Which stream should I pick first?**
A: Support & SLA. Low effort, immediate revenue, validates customer demand.

**Q: How much will this cost to launch?**
A: ~$80-200/month for tools. Mostly your time.

**Q: Can I do this part-time?**
A: Yes, at least for first 6 months. 20-40 hours/week to start.

**Q: What if a stream isn't working?**
A: Kill it and redeploy resources. Not all will work equally well.

**Q: How do I know if it's working?**
A: Track MRR weekly. If not growing 5-10% monthly, iterate or pivot.
